Match Pairs

Play!

Score:

Memory Single player
updated 22/03/2023

Instructions

Objective: Get all the pairs with the minimum number of tries.

A set of cards presented upside-down will be displayed on the screen. They can be flipped by clicking on them with the left-button of the mouse, and an image will be shown. You will choose two cards to flip, and if they have the same image, they will disappear from the screen and you will gain points. If they do not match, then you will not gain points and the cards will be flipped again.
The game finishes when all the cards are flipped. Good luck!
