Quick Quiz

Quick quiz is a multiple-choice quiz that consists of 10 questions, and for each question, there are four options to choose from. The quiz covers a wide range of topics. The goal of the quiz is to test the user's knowledge by selecting the correct answer out of the four options provided. After answering all the questions, the user's score is calculated based on the number of correct answers, each one with a value of 10 points. User's score can be saved to appear in the highscore leaderboard.

How to play?

Quick Quiz - Instructions

  1. To begin the quiz, click on the "Play" button.
  2. Read each question carefully and review all of the answer options before selecting the answer that you think is correct.
  3. To select your answer, click on the option that you believe is correct.
  4. Once you have selected your answer you will know if the answer was correct or not. In case it is incorrect, you will see the correct answer.
  5. Click on the "Next" button to go to the next question.
  6. Once you have answer all 10 questions, the quiz will give you your score.
  7. Save or not your score by introducing your name an clicking on the "Save" button to appear in the highscore leaderboard.
  8. If you would like to play the quiz again, you can do so by clicking on the "Play Again" button.
  9. Check the highscore leaderboard by clicking on the "High Scores" button.
  10. Have fun playing the quiz!
